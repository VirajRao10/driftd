Help

Driftd Support

Need help with Driftd? Send a note to support@virajrao.com.

Beta install

Driftd is currently distributed through TestFlight. Install Apple's TestFlight app, open the Driftd beta link, then tap Install. If the beta is full or unavailable, try again later or contact support.

Quick tips

Hold the screen to reveal the aim guide. Release when the guide points toward the next orbit node. Land close to the node to keep your chain alive, earn Credits, and build combo.

Progress

Your progress is saved on your device and may sync through Apple's iCloud key-value storage when iCloud is available. You can reset local progress from Driftd settings.

DriftPass Premium

DriftPass Premium is an optional auto-renewable subscription for premium cosmetic rewards while the subscription is active. You can restore purchases from Driftd settings. To manage or cancel the subscription, open your Apple ID subscriptions in iOS Settings. Driftd uses Apple's standard Terms of Use.

Troubleshooting

  • If premium rewards do not unlock, use Restore Purchases in Driftd settings.
  • If progress looks stale on another device, confirm iCloud is enabled and reopen Driftd on both devices.
  • If reminders are too frequent, turn them off in Driftd settings or iOS Settings.

Notifications

Daily challenge and streak reminders are optional. They can be disabled in Driftd settings or in iOS Settings.

Accessibility

Driftd includes reduced motion, tap-only controls, high contrast, sound, and haptic settings.
