Driftd Support

Need help with Driftd? Send a note to support@virajrao.com.

Quick tips

Hold the screen to reveal the aim guide. Release when the guide points toward the next orbit node. Land close to the node to keep your chain alive, earn Credits, and build combo.

Progress

Your progress is saved on your device and may sync through Apple's iCloud key-value storage when iCloud is available. You can reset local progress from Driftd settings.

Notifications

Daily challenge and streak reminders are optional. They can be disabled in Driftd settings or in iOS Settings.

Accessibility

Driftd includes reduced motion, tap-only controls, high contrast, sound, and haptic settings.
