Built for small daily sessions

Simple rules, sharp little decisions

Driftd keeps the controls quiet and lets the run build through timing, modifiers, rewards, and the choice to risk one more orbit.

Hold, aim, release

Hold to reveal the aim guide, release along the tangent, and catch the next node before the drift slips away.

Daily drift

Fresh goals and gameplay twists give each day a small ritual: perfects, combos, survival, Credits, or calm runs.

DriftPass

Level up through play to unlock power-ups and cosmetics, with optional Premium DriftPass rewards for subscribers.

Loadouts

Equip two modifiers at a time, from slower orbits and feather arcs to perfect bonuses and near-miss saves.

Personal style

Unlock orb skins, trails, and themes using Credits earned from play.

Comfort controls

Driftd includes haptics, sound, reduced motion, tap-only controls, high contrast, and optional reminders.

DriftPass

A reward track for better daily runs

DriftPass gives each level a little more shape: Credits, power-ups, orb skins, trails, and themes earned through regular play. Premium DriftPass adds subscription-only cosmetics while the subscription is active.

  • Free and Premium reward tracks
  • Cosmetics tied to level progress
  • Premium rewards managed through Apple subscriptions
